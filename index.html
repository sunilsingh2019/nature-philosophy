Up for the challenge?

Are you ready to transform your connection to nature, break free from boredom and dramatically boost your well-being?

The 7 Day Sit Spot Challenge is designed to break open this magic for you!

Are you craving to know you belong to the land that surrounds you?

The simple practise of a Sit Spot is a personal revolution which deeply meets your needs as an awake human.

Sit Spots settle your nervous system and fuel a powerful boost to your overall well-being.

Its also just absolutely amazing when you slow down enough for Nature to let you into HER world.

A brave new world of earth magic opens to you as you become intimate with the birds, the plants, the elements and the land itself.

Intimately observe the flow of Nature and deeply reconnect to the wisdom of the natural world.

What do i need to complete the challenge?

Ultimately, youll need just 3 things:

Commitment

If you bring the commitment and the time, we will provide the tools!

Time

Once you register we will be in touch to help you find your perfect location. This is where you will be sitting for 60 minutes, over a series of 7 days.

The Tools

Well then coach and guide you, day by day, as you learn to take your Sit Spot practice deeply into the natural world.

Our Sit Spot Challenge is designed to help you get nature connected during these times of social isolation.

So any safe and comfortable spot close to/in a natural place is perfect.

Once you have your special spot we will teach you powerful Deep Nature Connection practises which last for a life time.

The Nature Philosophy Sit Spot Challenge is a week long nature based transformation!
